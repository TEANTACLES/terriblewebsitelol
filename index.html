Where I was born

I was born in Waco, Texas on March 5th, 1992 and raised in Waco all my life. At the age of 19 I briefly moved in with friends in Phoenix, Arizona but that didn't last longer than a few months due to interpersonal issues. Outside of Phoenix and Texas, I've only ever visited a small town in Mexico and Northern California, but I'm hoping once I finish school and the Covid pandemic is finished I can finally travel and gain some good experiences.

My Hobbies

I'm very nerdy, mostly a homebody. My hobbies are building computers, playing videogames, and I recently picked up cooking. I'm not particularly good at cooking yet, I find the trickiest bit is learning timing and heat, everything else is prep and rather easy, just a bit laborious.
