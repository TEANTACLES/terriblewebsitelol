My favorite shows:

Ghost In The Shell: Stand Alone Complex
Running from 2002 to 2005, Ghost In The Shell: Stand Alone Complex is about a group of militarized, classified cybernetic police officers working for the Japanese government on stealth operations. The show regularly dealt with various high level political and societal concepts. It's considered a masterpiece and would later be followed up with 2 sequel series.
Evil
Evil is about a skeptical forensic psychologist hired by the Catholic church to investigate all manner of potentially supernatural events, determining if the church should get involved. The show is notable for addressing various social issues in each episode and applying a nuanced worldview to its subject matter. Evil began airing in 2019 and is currently wrapping up its second season, and greenlit for a third.
Doom Patrol
Doom Patrol follows the titular group, a ragtag band of traumatized, broken people with superpowers as they routinely find themselves in absurd situations, forced to be heroes despite their pain. The show started airing on the former streaming platform DC Universe in 2019, and later moved to HBO MAX for its second and third seasons. The show is also notable as being one of Brendon Fraser's biggest acting roles in over a decade.
Marianne
Marianne is a French horror television show that aired for a single season in 2019. It revolves around a novelist who realizes that there are uncanny and unnatural connections between the characters she writes in her novels and people in her home town, leading into a Satanic mystery than threatens to undo everything she knows and loves. The show was cancelled after one season.
Harley Quinn: The Animated Series
The show follows Harley Quinn as she breaks up with the Joker and strikes out on her own to make a name for herself as a Villain in her own right. The show began airing in 2019 and is due for a third season to air this fall. Like Doom Patrol, it began on DC Universe and moved to HBO Max when the service was shuttered.
